Assignment Name : ML Idea Generator 
Description : Suggest ML problems in college, healthcare, shopping and describe input → output.

1. College – Student Performance Prediction
Problem: Predict whether a student will perform well in exams.
Input: Attendance, study hours, previous marks, assignment scores.
Output: Predicted student performance (High / Medium / Low marks).

2. Healthcare – Disease Prediction
Problem: Predict the possibility of a disease based on patient data.
Input: Age, symptoms, medical history, blood pressure, test results.
Output: Disease risk prediction (Healthy / Risk of disease).

3. Shopping – Product Recommendation System
Problem: Suggest products to customers based on their interests.
Input: User browsing history, previous purchases, product ratings.
Output: Recommended products the user may like.

4. College – Attendance Monitoring System
Problem: Predict students who may have low attendance.
Input: Daily attendance records, class participation, schedule.
Output: Alert for students at risk of low attendance.

5. Healthcare – Hospital Appointment Prediction
Problem: Predict patient waiting time or appointment availability.
Input: Number of patients, doctor availability, appointment schedule.
Output: Estimated waiting time or recommended appointment slot.

6. Shopping – Customer Purchase Prediction
Problem: Predict whether a customer will buy a product.
Input: Customer demographics, browsing behavior, product price.
Output: Purchase likelihood (Yes / No).
